Keeping track of your case

Please create a password to use to check your case status. Your password cannot be reset if lost or forgotten, it is not shared with SafeHer India or participating companies. Your password must be at least 10 characters long.

You will be given a case number once you finish creating your case. Do not lose your case number or your password, you will not be able to look up your case by any other means.

We can send you an email with your case number if you would like. To protect your privacy, your email address will not be stored on our servers or shared with anyone (including participating companies).
